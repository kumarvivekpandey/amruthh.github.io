Our Story


Our commitment is to provide a high quality Jaggery which is not only tasty but is manufactured hygienically and processed without chemicals. The product is a result of extensive R&D, which have a lot of health benefits ensuring that our customers get nothing but the best. We aim to accomplish an Eco friendly and affordable product which can benefit all our consumers.

Mission and vision

While venturing into the Indian Jaggery market, Amruth mission and vision are :
  • To facilitate mass urban population for getting quality organic Jaggery to lead a healthy life.
  • To be the major brand player in Organic Brand market within one financial year within its set distribution territory.
